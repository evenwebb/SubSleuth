Browser vs CLI This web app is the default way to use SubSleuth — wizard, sorting, filters, and exports with no install. The Python CLI in the repo is for power users: batch CSV/HTML to disk, --compare-to for older exports, and automation.
What you need from Google Takeout A zip containing your YouTube watch history and subscriptions. Both are required for the best results.
  1. 1
    Open Google Takeout Go to takeout.google.com and sign in with the Google account linked to your YouTube.
  2. 2
    Deselect all, then select only YouTube and YouTube Music Click "Deselect all", then check YouTube and YouTube Music. Other products are not needed.
  3. 3
    Choose the right formats Under YouTube, make sure history is set to JSON or HTML, and subscriptions is set to CSV. Defaults usually work.
  4. 4
    Create export and wait for the download link Click "Next step", choose delivery method (download link is fine), then "Create export". Google emails you when the zip is ready — this can take minutes to hours.
  5. 5
    Download the zip to your computer Save the file locally. You'll upload it in the next step — do not unzip it first.
